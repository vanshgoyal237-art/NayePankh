About Us

Think Global,
Act Local.

NayePankh Foundation is a non-governmental organisation with a strong desire to help society and make it a better place for all. We are one of the biggest student-led NGOs of India with operations in Kanpur, Ghaziabad, and various other cities.

From free food and sanitary pads to clothing and education — we believe service to mankind is service to God. Affiliated with NITI Aayog, registered under the Indian Society Act, 1860.

"Let's revolutionise the society together — one person at a time."

What We Do

Our Core Programs

Every initiative is designed to create lasting impact in communities we serve.

📚

Education Drive

Free educational resources, tutoring and mentorship to underprivileged children — building foundations for a better future.

🍛

Food Distribution

Fighting hunger one meal at a time — distributing food to underprivileged families and stray animals alike.

🌸

Hygiene & Health

Sanitary napkins and awareness campaigns on personal hygiene, especially for young women in underserved areas.

👗

Clothing Aid

Providing clothing to families in need during winter drives — ensuring warmth and dignity for everyone.

💼

Internship Program

Empowering 30,000+ students with skills through purpose-driven internships in tech, design, and social impact.

🤝

Community Outreach

Bridging youth volunteers and communities in need through regular drives, events and awareness campaigns.

Our Founder

A Vision That
Changed Lives

"If we all do something, then together there is no problem that we cannot solve!"

Under Prashant's leadership, NayePankh has grown from a small volunteer group into one of India's largest student-led NGOs — touching over 2 lakh lives across multiple cities and training 30,000+ interns in the process.

All efforts are made possible only because of your support. Donations are tax exempted under 80G of the Indian Income Tax Act.
